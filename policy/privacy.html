privacy policy - eclipse

last updated: october 14, 2025

welcome to eclipse, a website designed to help you learn html and web development. this privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. eclipse is proudly created and hosted via github pages.

1. information we collect

when you use eclipse, we may collect:

  • account info: username, email, and password (stored securely).
  • learning progress: what lessons or tutorials you complete.
  • technical data: browser type, device info, and general usage stats.
  • optional data: anything you share through contact forms or feedback.

2. how we use your info

we use this info to:

  • keep your account working properly.
  • personalize your learning experience.
  • maintain and improve eclipse’s performance.
  • send important updates or replies if you contact us.
  • make sure everything stays secure.

3. keeping your info safe

we use encryption, secure connections, and github’s built-in safety features to protect your data. since eclipse runs on github pages, no sensitive server-side data, like payment info, is collected or stored.

4. sharing your info

we don’t sell, rent, or share your info with anyone outside of what’s needed to host and operate eclipse. github may collect some anonymous usage data, like page views, as part of their normal service.

5. your rights

you can:

  • ask for a copy of your data.
  • request deletion of your info or account.
  • fix any incorrect details.
  • opt out of future communications.

just email us at eclipsereal@proton.me to make it happen.

6. cookies

eclipse uses cookies to remember login sessions, progress, and preferences. you can block or delete cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly afterward.

7. updates to this policy

we’ll update this policy if anything changes, so check back here for the latest version. the “last updated” date at the top shows when the latest changes were made.

8. contact us

if you have questions about privacy or data, we’re here to help.
